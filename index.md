# SHA-4 Initiative

*Toward a standard for arithmetisation-oriented hash functions.*

A community effort supporting the opening of a standardisation process for arithmetisation-oriented (AO) hash functions. The work is open and deliberative, drawing on input from cryptographers, standards bodies, and practitioners building systems that rely on circuit-efficient hashing. It does not advocate for any specific candidate construction.

## Current work

- **[Open letter](letter)** — a call for NIST and the community to begin a pre-competition phase on AO hash standardisation. [Add your name.](letter#how-to-sign)
- **[Discussion note](docs/discussion-note)** — the longer technical argument behind the letter.
- **[GitHub repository](https://github.com/sha4-initiative)** — drafts, data, and issues.
